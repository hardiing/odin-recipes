Cheese Omelette

A basic omelette, filled with cheese.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 serving of shredded cheese
  • .5 tbsp salted butter
  • Salt/Pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp water

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, whisk
  2. Add water, salt, and pepper, then whisk
  3. Melt butter in pan over medium heat
  4. Pour egg mixture into pan
  5. Let the bottom of the egg cook
  6. Use spatula to focus on edges of egg, pouring excess egg underneath
  7. When there is minimal excess egg on top, add cheese and fold over omelette
  8. Plate the omelette and let the heat melt the cheese
