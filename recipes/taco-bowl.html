Taco Bowl Meal Prep

A simple taco bowl recipe for your meal prepping. Recipes is for five servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 2 packets of taco seasoning
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can whole kernel corn

Steps

  1. Cook rice in two cups of water
  2. Drain beans and corn and split into five containers
  3. Brown ground beef, then drain excess grease
  4. Add taco seasoning to beef, following instructions
  5. Add beef and rice, split into five portions, to containers
