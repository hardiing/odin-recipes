Bacon-Wrapped Smokies

Lil' smokies wrapped in bacon, coated with brown sugar. A great snack to round out a day's diet.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack bacon, cut into thirds
  • 1 pack lil smokies
  • Brown sugar
  • Toothpicks

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Wrap a piece of bacon around every smokie, using a toothpick to keep it together
  3. Cover wrapped smokies in brown sugar, to preference
  4. Bake in oven for 40 minutes
